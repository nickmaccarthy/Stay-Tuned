Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

Stay Tuned is designed with your privacy as a priority. We believe that a guitar tuner should do one thing exceptionally well: help you tune your instrument. That's why we've built our app to work entirely on your device, without collecting any personal data.

üîí Simple Privacy Promise: Stay Tuned does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Your audio never leaves your device.

Microphone Access

Stay Tuned requires access to your device's microphone to detect the pitch of your instrument. This is essential for the core tuning functionality.

How we use microphone access:

  • Audio is processed in real-time, entirely on your device
  • No audio recordings are created or stored
  • Audio data is never transmitted to any server
  • Microphone access is only active when the app is open and in use

Data Collection

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Audio recordings or microphone data
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Any information that could identify you

Local Storage

Stay Tuned stores your preferences (such as your preferred reference pitch and last selected tuning) locally on your device using iOS's standard storage mechanisms. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

Stay Tuned does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not contain any ads.

If you choose to make a tip through our Tip Jar feature, you'll be redirected to external payment services (Venmo, Cash App, or PayPal). These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Children's Privacy

Stay Tuned does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Given our commitment to not collecting data, we do not anticipate significant changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please reach out through our Support page.
