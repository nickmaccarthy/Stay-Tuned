About the App

Stay Tuned is a professional-grade guitar tuner built for musicians who demand precision and beauty. Featuring a stunning glassmorphism interface, real-time pitch detection powered by advanced signal processing, and support for multiple instruments and tunings.

Whether you're a beginner learning your first chords or a professional preparing for a gig, Stay Tuned provides the accuracy and reliability you need to sound your best.
